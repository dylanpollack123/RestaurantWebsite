POLLACK's SPORTS BAR & GRILL

Mondays

Bingo Night
7PM-10PM

Bring in your friends and family for a chance to win unique prizes. Prizes will change every week but will include gift cards, souveniers, and more! Bingo will start at 7PM sharp and wrap up at 10PM. Hope to see you there!

Tuesdays

Burger Night
8PM-12AM

Come on in for our juicy, delicous burgers for only $5! All burgers include fries OR tater tots and are cooked to your liking. Burgers will begin being served at 8PM sharp and wrap up at 12AM. Hope to see you there!

Wednesdays

Wing Wednesdays
8PM-12AM

Join us on Wednesdays for 50 cent wings. Any one of our 10 flavors will be available either traditional or boneless. Wings will begin being served at 8PM sharp and wrap up at 12AM. Hope to see you there!

Thursdays

Karaoke Night
9PM-1AM

Thursdays are for having fun and singing your lungs out. Our DJ will play any song of your choice that is available on Apple music. Bring your friends and reserve your time slot to sing starting at 9PM and ending at 1AM. Hope to see you there!

Weekends

Live Music
All Night

Friday through Sunday is for some good ole live music. With a variety of genres and new bands each weekend, come in and enjoy the sounds of good music while surrounded by good people. Live music will be from open to close. Hope to see you there!
